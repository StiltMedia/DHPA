FAQ

1. HOW MUCH DOES IT COST TO HIRE A DHPA PHOTOGRAPHER?

Please contact our bookings department directly by emailing info@dhpa.com or by calling our office at 1-212-924-4250.

2. WHY CAN'T I VIEW CERTAIN EVENTS?

Some events DHPA shoots are private on the site at the request of the client. Only clients who have been given specific permissions will be able to view these private events.

3. MY NAME IS SPELLED INCORRECTLY ON DHPA...HOW CAN I GET THAT FIXED?

Sorry about that! Our photographer must have heard you incorrectly. Please send correct spelling(s) along with the Image ID #(s) associated with the photo(s) Email Support@dhpa.com.

4. HOW DO I HIRE DHPA FOR AN EVENT I AM PLANNING?

Please visit the bookings link on our site or email Bookings@bfa.com.

5. DOES DHPA OFFER VIDEO SERVICES?

Totes. We have an amazing video team. Please email Bookings@BFA.com for examples and package rates.

6. DOES DHPA SHOOT ON FILM OR DIGITAL?

Digital. All BFA photographers shoot with high-end professional DSL camera systems.

7. CAN I PUBLISH DHPA IMAGES ON MY WEBSITE, IN MY PRINT MAGAZINE OR ON MY BLOG?

As a professional photo agency we require that you retain the proper usage rights before posting or printing our photos. We offer a range of automated licensing options for direct download from our site including: Web Usage (72 DPI) and Print Usage (300 DPI).

8. HOW MUCH DO DHPA IMAGES COST TO LICENSE?

Our rates are generated through the download pricing module which will appear when the Purchase button is pressed below a thumbnail photo on the site.

9. DOES DHPA REPRESENT ACTORS OR MODELS?

Nopers. We are an event photography agency and do not represent the talent that we shoot.

10. DOES dHPA HAVE PHOTOGRAPHERS IN LOS ANGELES OR ANY OTHER CITY BESIDES NEW YORK CITY?

Yes. DHPA has recently expanded to the Los Angeles area. Please inquire about our global network of associate photographers as well.

11. CAN I HIRE A DHPA PHOTOGRAPHER TO TRAVEL OVERSEAS?

Totes. Will travel.

12. IS THERE AN HOURLY MINIMUM TO HIRE A BFA PHOTOGRAPHER?

No, BUT a higher hourly rate will apply for bookings requiring one hour or less.

13. HOW DO I BECOME A DHPA PHOTOGRAPHER?

Send your resume to info@dhpa.com.

14. CAN I HIRE A DHPA PHOTOGRAPHER FOR SOCIAL MEDIA PURPOSES ONLY?

Yes you can.

15. WHEN I HIRE DHPA TO SHOOT AN EVENT, DO I OWN THE COPYRIGHT OF THOSE PHOTOS?

Sorry Charlie, DHPA retains all copyright on the images taken by it's photographers at events.

16. IF I HIRE DHPA FOR AN EVENT, CAN I SEND THE HIGH RESOLUTION IMAGES TO PRESS OUTLETS TO RUN FOR FREE?

Not unless you purchase PR Usage rights.

17. HOW DO I DOWNLOAD MY EVENT?

If you have hired DHPA for your event you will be emailed permissions to view and download accordingly once the event is posted to the site.

18. DO I HAVE TO SEND A MESSENGER TO GRAB A DISK OF IMAGES THE NEXT DAY AFTER MY EVENT?

Welcome to the future...download directly from our site no matter where you are. We will send you an email with clear instructions. Avoid the hassle of sending a messenger.

19. IS THERE A WAY TO HAVE MY PHOTO(S) REMOVED FROM THE DHPA WEBSITE?

Sure but why wouldn't you want the world to see you in a gorilla suit doing Svedka shots off your best buddy's bottom?! But for serious...email us if you have any such emergencies. ...and please supply the applicable Image ID #(s).
